Your children’s future success is highly dependent on their ability to read and understand very well the materials that they study, and their capability to communicate their ideas clearly and effectively through writing. We at R.E.A.D. – or Reading Enhancement and Academic Development – are here to help you help your children be the best readers and writers they can be and in the process, be the best students they can be. And when they’re able to do that, success in life can’t be far behind.
